Crystal Collector!

Rules of the Game

1) You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

2) There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

3) You win the game by matching your total score to the random game number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

4) The value of each jewel is hidden from you until you click on it.

5) After each round the value of each crystal will change, the loss or win counter will go up, and the random number will reset to something different. Good luck!
